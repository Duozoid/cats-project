PUMA AND CHEETAH

The puma line includes only two genera - the puma itself (Puma) and the cheetah (Acinonyx). There are only two pumas left, the others are extinct - the puma (Puma concolor) and the jaguarundi (Puma yagouaroundi). The yagouaroundi, although more like a weasel, is a real cat!
